## A note on seasonal travel
Colorado conditions can change quickly, especially in the mountains.

Keep in mind:
- Afternoon thunderstorms are common in summer.
- Snowstorms can occur from fall through spring.
- Wind can be significant at higher elevations.
- Cell service may be limited in canyons and parks.

Before heading out:
- Check weather and road conditions.
- Carry water, layers, and sun protection.
- Allow extra time for return travel.
