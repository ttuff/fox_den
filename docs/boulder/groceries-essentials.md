# Groceries & essentials

Nearby spots for staples, snacks, and quick errands.

## Quick version
- Full-size grocers on 28th Street corridor; natural markets throughout town.
- Convenience stores near Pearl and University Hill for late-night basics.
- Pharmacies clustered along 28th/Broadway.
- Farmers markets operate seasonally downtown (check schedule).

## Grocery options
- Mainstream supermarkets on 28th and Arapahoe corridors for full carts.
- Natural/organic markets scattered across town with solid prepared foods.
- Warehouse clubs and specialty Asian/Latin markets available along 28th.

## Essentials & pharmacies
- Drugstores on Broadway and 28th for prescriptions, toiletries, and travel items.
- Outdoor gear shops near Pearl and Canyon if you forgot layers—see (outdoors.md) for trail prep.

## Farmers markets & quick bites
- Seasonal farmers market downtown (typically Saturdays, spring–fall) with produce and food stalls.
- Food trucks and carts pop up around Pearl Street during events.

## Delivery & pickups
- Major grocers support curbside pickup and delivery; schedule ahead during storms or holidays.
- Many convenience stores are on delivery apps for snacks and drinks.
