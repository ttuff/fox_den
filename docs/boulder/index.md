## One-screen essentials

- Altitude is real—hydrate and take it slow on day one.
- Weather changes fast; layers beat bulk.
- Sun is strong: sunscreen and hats help.
- Plan transport early when events are in town.
- Early starts make popular trails calmer.
- Keep reusable bags handy for groceries.
