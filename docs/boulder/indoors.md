# Things to do – indoors

While Boulder is famous for the outdoors, there’s plenty to do inside — especially on windy days, during snowstorms, wildfire smoke events, or when you just want something low-key. This page highlights indoor options for families, fitness-minded guests, and anyone looking for culture or entertainment.

## Indoor activities for kids & families
Great options for younger visitors or family downtime.
- **[WOW! Children’s Museum](https://wowchildrensmuseum.org)** – Hands-on exhibits, art, and play spaces for younger kids.
- **[Jump City](https://jumpcityus.com)** – Indoor trampoline park — high-energy and popular for school-age kids.
- **[Gateway Fun Center](https://www.gatewayfunpark.com)** – Arcade games, indoor attractions, and casual entertainment.

These are especially helpful on cold, snowy, or smoky days.

## Climbing gyms (very Boulder)
Boulder has a strong indoor climbing scene, from beginner-friendly gyms to elite training facilities.
- **[Movement Climbing + Fitness](https://movementclimbing.com)** – Large facility with bouldering, roped climbing, yoga, and fitness classes.
- **[The Spot Boulder](https://thespotboulder.com)** – Bouldering-focused gym with a strong local community vibe.

Great alternatives when weather or trail conditions aren’t ideal.

## Gyms, pools & indoor fitness
For workouts beyond climbing.
- **[East Boulder Community Center](https://bouldercolorado.gov/services/east-boulder-community-center)** – Indoor pools, gym, fitness classes, and family facilities.
- **[North Boulder Recreation Center](https://bouldercolorado.gov/services/north-boulder-rec-center)** – Pool, weight room, and fitness options.
- **Boulder Indoor Soccer** – Indoor sports leagues and open play options.

## Yoga, Pilates & mind-body studios
Boulder is known for its wellness culture.
- **Yoga Pod** – Heated and non-heated classes; multiple locations.
- **CorePower Yoga** – High-energy classes with many daily options.
- **TruFusion Boulder** – Variety of classes including yoga, strength, and conditioning.

## Movies & indoor entertainment
- **[Century Boulder](https://www.cinemark.com/theatres/co-boulder/century-boulder)** – Mainstream movie theater option.
- **Boedecker Theater** – Independent films, documentaries, and curated programming (check schedule).

## Museums, culture & quiet indoor time
- **[Boulder Museum of Contemporary Art](https://bmoca.org)** – Small but thoughtful exhibitions, often featuring regional artists.
- **[Museum of Boulder](https://museumofboulder.org)** – Local history, rotating exhibits, and a good introduction to Boulder’s past and present.

## Live music & performances (indoors)
Boulder has a strong indoor music and performance scene.
- **[Boulder Theater](https://bouldertheater.com)** – National and regional touring acts.
- **[Fox Theatre](https://foxtheatre.com)** – Popular for concerts and DJ shows.
- **Chautauqua Auditorium** – Lectures, concerts, and special events (seasonal).

## A local tip
Indoor spots fill up quickly during snowstorms, high-wind days, wildfire smoke events, Sundance Film Festival, and school breaks or weekends. Checking hours and booking classes or tickets ahead of time can save frustration.

If you’d like recommendations based on kids’ ages, fitness goals, quiet vs high-energy, or current weather conditions, just ask — we’re happy to help.
