# Checkout

Simple steps for an easy departure.

## Quick version
- **Checkout time:** Checkout by: : AM (message us if you need flexibility).
- **Dishes:** Load the dishwasher and run it if needed, or wash any remaining dishes.
- **Trash & recycling:** Place trash and recycling in the appropriate bins. Bins located: __________________________.
- **Towels & linens:** Leave used towels in the __________________. No need to strip beds unless noted.
- **Lights & thermostat:** Turn off lights and set thermostat to a reasonable setting.
- **Locking up:** Close and lock all doors and windows. Return keys to __________________ or secure the lockbox as instructed. Double-check the front and back doors.
- **Final look:** Check outlets for chargers or personal items; make sure nothing is left behind.

## Details
- **Kitchen:** Load and start the dishwasher with any remaining dishes. Wipe counters and toss perishable food.
- **Trash, recycling, and compost:** Sort into the appropriate bins and place them in their locations. If your stay overlaps a pickup day, feel free to put bins out if convenient (never required).
- **Laundry:** Leave used towels in the designated spot; no need to strip beds unless noted in your booking.
- **Climate:** Set thermostat to a reasonable temp for the season and turn off portable heaters or fans.
- **Keys/access:** Ensure the smart lock is fully engaged and any spare keys/fobs are returned as instructed.
